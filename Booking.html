Online Event Booking Without the Hassle

InviteBIG enables event organizers to create, organize and book events in real-time.

Find Bars & Nightclubs

These venues offer a fun, high-energy atmosphere that is great for booking birthday parties, bachelor / bachelorette festivities and girls/guys nights out.

Find Conference & Meeting Rooms

If you are planning a conference, wedding or any corporate event, booking a Conference Centers or Meeting Rooms online is the way to go.

Find Concert & Music Halls

If you're looking to book a venue for a fundraiser, concert or show, these venues are ideal. Larger in size and staffed to accommodate the needs of bigger groups, they're perfect for events where live music is a factor.

Find Fun & Amusement Centers

Organizing a children's party or just a lighthearted event for a group of friends? These bowling alleys, arcades and family fun centers are great options for making sure everyone in attendance has a fantastic time.
