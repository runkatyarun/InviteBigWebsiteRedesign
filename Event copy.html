Sync up with your favorite online calendars and automatically update availability.

Whether you are most comfortable using Google Calendar, Outlook or any other major calendar service to manage your schedule, we can sync up with the online calendar of your choice. Our application will automatically update your venue’s availability, along with event schedule, in real-time. In other words, you’ll never have to worry about maintaining two calendars; we’ll take care of it for you.

Accept payments, deposits & offer refunds online.

InviteBIG securely accepts online payments, including deposits. We use only the most trusted and secure payment processing services, making it possible for your business to accept all major credit cards. Funds are held in our safe escrow account until the event takes place, at which point, they’ll be released to your venue either via check or your bank account. From there, keep track of all your transactions and revenue, within your dashboard, using our accounting software.

Manage all your bookings and schedule in one place

Whether a booking comes in over the phone, through email or via InviteBIG.com, you’ll be able to input and manage all incoming bookings through our website. Plus, with our all-in-one availability calendar, you and your customers will see exactly which resources and spaces are available without having to toggle between multiple calendars. This will cut back on double bookings and duplicate entries. We are also working to give venues the ability to manage event itineraries, customer requests and employee schedules, in addition to bookings, all in one convenient place.
